Fridge Manager Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

English

Fridge Manager does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app is designed to help users manage fridge items, expiration dates, shopping lists, and recipes on their device.

  • No user account is required.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No analytics SDKs are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • No tracking technologies are used.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
